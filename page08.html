WisdomPen Update: 0.8

New Improvements

-New Ui
-Model Upgrade to gpt o3-mini (medium)
-Rete limits to 28 requests per day (max)
-Image now supported (input)
-Quality of life bug fixes
-Little faster responses (some cases)
-Overall better

With this new update, we are excited to announce that we are now in the process of making WisdomPen a more reliable and accurate source of Islamic knowledge. Which is important as we get closer to the launch of WisdomPen to the public. Update 0.8 is a significant step towards achieving this goal. As gpt o3-mini (medium) is a more advanced model that can provide more relevant responses to user queries. And also we have seen less helucinations and more accurate responses. Its important the user has close to 0 percent made up sources and information from the model. And also with this update we are also adding rate limits to our service as this will help us to manage the load on our servers and ensure that all users have access to the service, for free, our commitment to providing a reliable and best Islamic knowledge AI is important and this step allows that. Users will get 28 requests per day, which is 840 requests per month. There will be noticeable delay in responses as model thinks before answering. But for simple questions may see little faster response. Ui is important for better experience, so there is a more simple easy ui which will enable all users accross different deviecs to get the best experience.

Beta testing for inconsistencies and bugs

Firstly, WisdomPen will provide sourced-based responses, ensuring that answers are backed up by evidence to authenticate but there will be times when ai would helucinations or be interprating information worng, we are strict in those mistakes so we want the beta testers to send us those responses through (Report a bug) in the chat ui. This will help in Improving the models responses. Also for any ui bugs like chat box not working or ai responses not being displayed or running out of request, send the issue with a sreenshot.

Vison/Hopes

INSHALLAH, we are hoping to make WisdomPen 0.8 a reliable and accurate source of Islamic knowledge. and 0.8 is a significant leap form non reasoning models, as we see less helucinations and more accurate responses. We see WisdomPen in 2027 or any Ai being more reliable then some scholars. our goal is not to replace scholars but to provide a tool for Muslims and non Muslims to get the best information about Islam.
INSHALLAH, WisdomPen is set to achive 70 percent accuracy this year. And 80 percent is the goal for us to make this public. Hopefully, we will be able to achieve this goal and provide a reliable and accurate source of Islamic knowledge for all.
